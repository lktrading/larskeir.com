Whether we are in a room full of traders or just ‘feeling them’ online, the truth is, they can influence us. 

Crowd mentality is in psychology described as opposed to individual mentality. It is irrational, unconscious, and easy to manipulate. What this means in trading is that if you get into the ‘zone’ of the crowd, which is, in fact, the opposite of "the zone," you may start to make decisions through emotions, not be able to think or make strategies, and be responsive to signals that are usually not in your best interest.
